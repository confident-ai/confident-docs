### Added

- **New Alerts Page** - The old view is gone; from the rubble we raised / a page where each alert lives all of its days. Every firing now carries its full backstory in tow— / what tripped, when it tripped, and how often it'd go— / so you drill from a symptom to root in a click, / find issues far faster and ghost-chase less thick. Consider yourself _alert_-ed.
- **Trace Exports** - Your traces have packed and are ready to roam, / now you pick the destination they get to call home. No screenshot smuggling, no copy-paste tolls— / just data that lands where the rest of it strolls. _Export_ control: yours.
- **Official Test Runs** - Mark a run as official and watch the noise fade— / the scratch runs and smoke tests no longer invade. Only the runs that should count get to count, / so your eval history tells truth, not amount. Make it _official_.
- **Salesforce & Snowflake as Knowledge Bases** - Salesforce and Snowflake have entered the fold / as sources for synthetic data, fresh-spun from gold. Point at the systems where real data resides, / and let the goldens write themselves on both sides. _Snow_ problem at all.
- **Attack Engine for Risk Assessment Schedules** - Your scheduled assessments no longer go light— / the full attack engine now runs through the night. On autopilot it strikes just as hard as by hand, / so threats meet their match whether watched or unmanned. Set it, forget it, _attack_ it.
- **Org-Level Client (Python & TypeScript)** - A brand-new client, in Python and TS, / that speaks to your org with one key—nothing less. Spin up fresh projects on the fly as you please, / provision at scale with programmatic ease. No console clicking, no setup to wage— / just call it and watch a new project take stage. The _key_ to the kingdom.
