No matter what plan you're on — Free, Starter, Premium, Team, or Enterprise — the Confident AI team is here to help. Whether you have a quick question, need help debugging an integration, or want hands-on guidance rolling out evaluations across your organization, we've got you covered.

## Your SOS Options

Every Confident AI user has access to the following support channels:

  </Card>

  <Card

## Team Support

Team plan customers get dedicated support channels with faster, SLA-backed support.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Slack or Teams" icon="fa-brands fa-slack">
    Get a shared Slack or Microsoft Teams channel with direct access to Confident AI support and engineering for fast questions and escalations.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Support Portal" icon="ticket">
    Raise and track support tickets directly in the application. Ticket response and handling follow your plan SLA.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
